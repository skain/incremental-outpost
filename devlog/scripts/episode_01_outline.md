*Focus:* Developing the core logic of the game.

*   **The Logic Gate:** Discussion of your transition from web development to gamedev logic. You start with robust, clean code for things like projectiles and collision because that's what you know—calculating values that must be perfect for the system to function.

*   **Developer Insight:** Highlight how your experience in scalable systems helped build a robust foundation, while the jump into gamedev taught you the necessity of "the extra bit" (visual feedback).
*   **Game Logic vs. Feel:** Use this section to show old codebase clips (high logic, low juice) compared to current ones.
